PROTEINS

Protein helps in muscle hypertrophy (growth) by providing amino acids necessary for
muscle repair after exercise. Protein reduces muscle soreness and helps tissues recover faster after workouts.

CARBOHYDRATES

Carbs provide quick energy. During workouts, muscles use stored carbs for fuel, particularly
in high-intensity exercises. After workouts, carbs help replenish glycogen stores,
which is especially important for those who exercise frequently or intensely.

FATS

Fats are a dense source of energy and can be used as fuel , allowing carbohydrates
to be saved for high-intensity efforts. Fats are essential for hormone production,which
is important for muscle growth and recovery.
